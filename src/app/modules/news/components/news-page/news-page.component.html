20.03.2023 ADM to offer BiOWiSH® Fertilizer Enhancement to improve nutrient efficiency

CHICAGO and CINCINNATI, OHIO—ADM (NYSE: ADM) and BiOWiSH Technologies, Inc. (BiOWiSH) today announced ADM will offer a BiOWiSH® Fertilizer Enhancement as an option to urea, monoammonium phosphate (MAP), diammonium phosphate (DAP) and NPK blends. The new product will be available from ADM Farm Direct Fertilizer as well as the company's wholesale business.
